Metal Roofing Options

Three Styles, One Promise: Lasting Protection

Each metal roofing system offers distinct aesthetics and performance characteristics. We'll help you choose the perfect match for your property.

Standing Seam

The premium choice for modern homes and commercial buildings. Standing seam panels feature raised interlocking seams with concealed fasteners — delivering a sleek, clean aesthetic and superior weather resistance.

  • Concealed fastener system — no exposed screws
  • Thermal expansion-friendly design
  • Wide range of colors and finishes
  • Ideal for both residential and commercial

Corrugated Metal

A classic profile with timeless appeal. Corrugated metal panels offer excellent strength-to-weight ratio and proven performance. Popular for farmhouses, modern homes, commercial buildings, and agricultural structures.

  • Cost-effective metal roofing option
  • Exceptional structural strength
  • Fast installation
  • Great for large-scale commercial projects

Stone-Coated Steel

The best of both worlds — the beauty of traditional tile, shakes, or shingles with the durability and longevity of steel. Stone-coated steel uses a layer of stone granules bonded to a steel base for a naturally textured appearance.

  • Mimics tile, shake, or shingle appearance
  • Class 4 impact resistance (highest rating)
  • Exceptional wind resistance (up to 120 mph)
  • Lightweight — no structural reinforcement needed
Why Metal

The Benefits of Metal Roofing

Metal roofing isn't just durable — it's a smart investment that pays dividends for decades.

50+ Year Lifespan

Metal roofs outlast asphalt by 3–4x. Many last 70+ years with minimal maintenance.

Energy Efficient

Reflects solar heat, reducing cooling costs by up to 25%. Many products are Energy Star certified.

Weather Resistant

Handles Minnesota's harshest conditions — heavy snow, ice dams, hail, and high winds up to 140 mph.

Low Maintenance

No cracking, shrinking, or eroding. Metal requires almost zero maintenance beyond occasional debris clearing.

Higher Home Value

Metal roofing increases property value and appeals to buyers who value durability and energy efficiency.

Eco-Friendly

100% recyclable at end of life. Many panels contain recycled content. Reduces landfill waste from tear-offs.
