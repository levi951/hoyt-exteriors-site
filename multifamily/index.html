Full Multifamily Exterior Services

We handle the full exterior envelope — roofing, siding, gutters, insulation, and windows. Every project starts with an AI-powered drone inspection so you have a complete picture before work begins.

Multifamily Roofing

Pitched roofs, flat sections, or both — we handle it. Full replacements, targeted repairs, storm damage restoration. Every job is documented thoroughly for your board, your insurance adjuster, and your capital planning file.

According to the GAF Master Elite® program, certified contractors must meet strict installation standards — Hoyt Exteriors maintains this certification.

Siding Replacement & Repair

Worn siding signals deferred maintenance to prospective tenants and drives up heating costs. We install fiber cement, vinyl, metal panel, and composite systems matched to your building’s performance requirements.

The ENERGY STAR® Seal and Insulate program reports that air sealing and insulation can reduce energy costs by up to 15%.

Gutters & Drainage

Undersized or failing gutters on a multifamily building create foundation issues, landscape damage, and exterior wall deterioration. We size and install commercial-grade systems built for your roof area and Minnesota’s precipitation load.

Insulation & Energy Performance

Our thermal imaging inspection identifies exactly where heat is escaping — attic bypasses, rim joists, roofline gaps. We fix it with the right system and you see it in your energy bills.

The U.S. Department of Energy estimates that proper insulation can reduce heating and cooling costs by 20% or more in northern climates.

Window Installation

Energy-efficient replacement windows across multifamily properties. Installed to minimize disruption to occupied units — residents barely notice we were there.

ENERGY STAR-certified windows can lower household energy bills by an average of 12%, per the EPA ENERGY STAR program.

How We Handle Occupied Buildings

We’ve done this enough to know what property managers need: a clear schedule, minimal tenant disruption, daily cleanup, and communication when something changes.

Before work starts, you get a project timeline by building and phase. Our trade partner network is experienced in occupied multifamily environments. We don’t leave a mess. We don’t go quiet mid-project. You won’t be chasing us for updates.

AI-Powered Drone Inspections for Multifamily

Every multifamily project starts with a drone inspection and thermal imaging assessment. You get a written report with photos, findings, and itemized scope — before we ask for anything.

That report isn’t just for us. Property managers use it for board presentations. Building owners use it for insurance claims. HOA boards use it for capital planning. It’s yours.
