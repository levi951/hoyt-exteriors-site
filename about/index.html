Our Story

Family Roots. Minnesota Built.

Hoyt Exteriors was built the old way — show up, do the work right, stand behind it. That's still the foundation. It always will be.

But we didn't stop there. While most contractors in the Twin Cities are still sending a guy with a ladder to eyeball your roof, we're flying drones. Running thermal imaging. Using AI-assisted analysis to find damage that standard inspections miss entirely. Documenting everything before, during, and after — so you have a complete record and zero surprises.

We're a family business with the feel of a local contractor and the tools of something much larger. That combination is exactly what our clients come back for.

Technology

We See What's Hidden

Our AI-powered fleet gives us an unfair advantage — and we pass that advantage straight to you. Every inspection is backed by data, not guesswork.

Drone Inspections

We deploy DJI Matrice 4T drones for thermal imaging and DJI Mavic 3E for high-resolution RGB photography. Every angle, every detail — captured before we ever set foot on your roof.

DJI Matrice 4T — Thermal DJI Mavic 3E — RGB

Thermal Imaging

Infrared thermal cameras reveal moisture intrusion, insulation gaps, and energy loss that the naked eye can't detect. We find problems before they become emergencies.

Moisture Detection Energy Loss Mapping

AI-Powered Fleet

Our AI-powered fleet processes inspection data, generates detailed reports, and identifies issues with precision that manual inspections simply can't match. Faster assessments, better outcomes.
