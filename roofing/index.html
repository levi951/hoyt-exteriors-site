Roofing Materials

The Right Roof for Every Structure

We install a full range of roofing materials — each selected for durability, aesthetics, and performance in Minnesota's demanding climate.

Asphalt Shingles

The Modern Choice

According to the GAF warranty program, certified installations can qualify for up to 50-year non-prorated coverage — the strongest warranty in residential roofing.

The most popular roofing material in America — and for good reason. Asphalt shingles offer exceptional versatility, a wide range of colors and styles, and reliable protection at an affordable price point. Available in architectural and designer profiles to complement any home.

Cedar Shakes

For Rustic Appeal

Nothing matches the timeless beauty of natural cedar. Cedar shake roofing delivers a warm, rustic aesthetic while providing excellent natural insulation. Each shake develops a distinctive silver-gray patina over time, adding character and curb appeal to your home.

Metal Roofing

Durable and Long-Lasting

The Metal Roofing Alliance reports metal roofs last 40–70 years — 2–3× the lifespan of asphalt — with Class A fire ratings standard.

The ultimate in roofing longevity. Metal roofing systems offer energy efficiency, exceptional durability, and a lifespan exceeding 50 years. Available in standing seam, corrugated, and stone-coated steel profiles — perfect for both residential and commercial applications.

Flat Roofs

Built for Commercial

Purpose-built systems for commercial and low-slope applications. We install premium flat roofing from Duro-Last and Mule Hide — trusted brands known for watertight performance and long-term reliability. Ideal for commercial buildings, multi-family properties, and modern residential designs.
