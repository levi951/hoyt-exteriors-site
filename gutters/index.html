Meet Galactic Gutter Guards

Our proprietary gutter protection system, engineered for Minnesota's toughest conditions. Built to handle heavy leaf fall, pine needles, ice dams, and torrential rain — so your gutters never clog again.

Micro-Mesh Filtration — Surgical-grade stainless steel mesh blocks everything but water. Leaves, pine needles, shingle grit — nothing gets through.
Ice Dam Resistant — Designed for Minnesota's freeze-thaw cycles. Prevents ice buildup inside gutters that leads to fascia rot and interior water damage.
High-Volume Flow — Handles 150+ inches of rainfall per hour. Your gutters work even in Minnesota's worst summer storms.
Professional Installation Only — Every Galactic guard is installed by our trained crews. No DIY kits, no shortcuts — just a system that works from day one.
No-Clog Guarantee — We stand behind Galactic Gutter Guards with a written performance guarantee. If they clog, we come back and fix it — free.
Low Profile Design — Nearly invisible from the ground. No bulky plastic hoods or ugly snap-on covers ruining your roofline.
