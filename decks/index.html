DECK MATERIALS

Built With the Best

Three distinct materials to match your vision, lifestyle, and budget. Each one engineered to withstand Minnesota's extreme seasons.

Cedar Decks

Natural beauty meets classic warmth. Western red cedar delivers rich, warm tones that age gracefully to a distinguished silver patina — or maintain their original glow with periodic sealing.

  • Natural resistance to rot, decay, and insects
  • Warm tones that complement any home style
  • Lightweight yet structurally strong
  • Eco-friendly and sustainably harvested

Western red cedar carries a natural Class B fire rating and is recommended by the American Wood Council for exterior applications in northern climates.

Hardwood / Ipe Decks

The pinnacle of deck materials. South American Ipe hardwood is three times harder than cedar, incredibly dense, and virtually impervious to weather, insects, and wear.

  • Density rivaling steel — won't scratch or dent
  • Natural oils resist moisture and mold
  • Lifespan exceeding 50 years
  • Rich chocolate-brown tones

Composite Decks

The best of both worlds — made from recycled plastic and reclaimed wood fibers, composite decking delivers the look of real wood with virtually zero maintenance.

  • No staining, sealing, or sanding — ever
  • Recycled materials — environmentally responsible
  • Fade, stain, and scratch resistant
  • Wide range of colors and textures

The 2025 Cost vs. Value Report shows composite deck additions recoup approximately 63% of their cost at resale — with significantly lower lifetime maintenance costs than wood.
