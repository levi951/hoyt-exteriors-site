FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our commercial roofing and exterior services.

We install three primary commercial roofing systems: TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) for flat and low-slope roofs, EPDM rubber membrane roofing for large-footprint buildings, and standing seam metal roofing for any slope. Each system is selected based on your building's architecture, usage, budget, and long-term performance goals. We're certified by GAF (Master Elite — top 1% of contractors) and CertainTeed, so every installation meets the highest manufacturer standards.
Timeline depends on the size and complexity of your building. A typical commercial roof replacement for a 10,000–20,000 sq ft building takes 1–3 weeks. Larger projects, such as warehouse or multi-building campuses, may take 4–8 weeks. We provide a detailed project timeline during the quoting phase, and our crews work efficiently to complete the project on schedule while maintaining safety and quality standards.
Absolutely. We understand that your business can't shut down for a roofing project. We routinely schedule work during off-hours, weekends, and phased sections to keep your operations running smoothly. For noise-sensitive environments like healthcare facilities and offices, we coordinate with your team to schedule the loudest work during low-traffic periods. Minimizing disruption is a core part of our commercial project planning.
Our building envelope assessment is a comprehensive evaluation of your entire exterior shell — roof, walls, windows, doors, and foundation interface. It includes a drone-based aerial inspection, infrared thermal imaging to detect moisture and insulation deficiencies, visual inspection of all flashing, caulking, and penetration points, and a detailed report with prioritized recommendations. You receive high-resolution photos, thermal maps, and cost estimates for any remediation needed.
Commercial thermal imaging typically ranges from $500–$2,500 depending on building size and scope. Small retail or office buildings start around $500, while large warehouses or multi-building campuses may be $1,500–$2,500. The investment pays for itself many times over — a single undetected moisture issue can cost $50,000+ in structural repairs. We also offer thermal imaging as part of maintenance contracts at a reduced rate. Contact us for a specific quote based on your property.
Yes. We have extensive experience navigating commercial insurance claims for storm damage, hail, wind, and water intrusion. Our process includes thorough damage documentation with drone photography, thermal imaging, and detailed written reports that insurance adjusters need. We work directly with your insurance company, attend adjuster meetings, and provide supplemental documentation when needed. Our thermal imaging and drone data often uncovers damage that visual inspections miss, which can significantly increase approved claim amounts.
Warranty options depend on the roofing system. TPO systems offer manufacturer warranties up to 30 years covering both materials and labor. EPDM systems also carry warranties up to 30 years. Metal roofing systems can be warranted for up to 50 years. In addition to manufacturer warranties, Hoyt Exteriors provides a 3-year workmanship warranty on all installations. As a GAF Master Elite contractor, we can also offer enhanced warranty options not available through standard installers.
Yes, we offer annual and semi-annual maintenance contracts for commercial properties. Our maintenance programs include scheduled inspections (visual and thermal), gutter cleaning, minor repairs, sealant and flashing checks, and detailed condition reports. Regular maintenance extends your roof's lifespan by 5–10 years on average and helps you budget predictably instead of facing unexpected emergency repairs. We tailor each contract to your building's specific needs and can bundle multiple properties for volume pricing.
