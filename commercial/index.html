AI-POWERED INSPECTIONS

We Fly Your Building Before We Touch It

Before we touch your building, we fly it. Drone captures high-resolution imagery of every square foot of your roof — including sections that are unsafe or impractical to walk. AI analysis identifies damage patterns, membrane issues, flashing gaps, and ponding areas that standard visual inspections miss.

Thermal imaging goes further. It finds moisture intrusion and heat loss beneath the surface — problems that look fine from above but are quietly driving up operating costs and eroding your building envelope.

You get a written report with findings, photos, and a clear scope of work. That report is yours — use it for board presentations, insurance claims, or capital planning. We ask for nothing until you've seen what we see.
