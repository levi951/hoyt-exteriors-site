YEAR-ROUND COMFORT

Your Attic Is the Key

Up to 40% of a home's energy loss escapes through the attic. When your attic insulation is thin, compressed, or unevenly distributed, your furnace and air conditioner work overtime — and you feel the difference in every room.

Winter Protection

Proper insulation keeps heated air inside your living spaces instead of escaping through the attic. Your furnace runs less, your rooms stay warmer, and ice dams become a thing of the past.

Summer Cooling

In summer, an under-insulated attic can exceed 150°F, radiating heat down into your living spaces. Proper insulation blocks that heat transfer, keeping upper floors cool.

Energy Savings

Reducing heat loss and gain means your HVAC system works less. Most homeowners see meaningful reductions in their energy bills — and the investment pays for itself within a few years.

Ice Dam Prevention

Proper attic insulation combined with adequate ventilation prevents the uneven roof temperatures that cause ice dams — one of the most common and damaging winter problems for Minnesota homes.
