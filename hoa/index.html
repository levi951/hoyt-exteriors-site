FAQ

Your Questions Answered

Do we need roof access for the drone?

+

No. The drone captures the entire roof from above; we never step on your shingles. All we need is the signed flight release and clear weather.

How long does the free scan take?

+

We schedule the flight within 48 hours of the signed release, and you receive the PDF within 24 hours after the flight. Most HOAs have results in hand within 3 business days.

Is the free scan really free? What's the catch?

+

No catch. The free scan includes the orthomosaic map, basic thermal snapshot, and a one-page findings report. We offer it because it's the best way to show you the value of proactive roof maintenance. If you want to go deeper, our paid add-ons are available — but there's zero obligation.

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve HOA communities across the Minneapolis-St. Paul metro and surrounding suburbs — Lakeville, Apple Valley, Eagan, Burnsville, Bloomington, Plymouth, Maple Grove, and beyond. If you're in the Twin Cities area, we can fly your property.

Do we need board approval before scheduling?

+

Yes — the flight release requires confirmation that the HOA Board has approved the inspection. We recommend bringing our free scan offer to your next board meeting. We can provide a one-page summary you can share with board members.
