What We Repair

Common Roofing Issues We Solve

Our experienced team diagnoses and fixes the full spectrum of roofing problems — from cosmetic wear to structural failures.

Curling Shingles

Shingles that curl, buckle, or lift compromise your roof's weather barrier. We replace affected areas with matching materials and address the underlying ventilation or moisture issues that caused the damage.

Leak Repair

Water intrusion is your roof's most dangerous enemy. We use thermal imaging and systematic inspection to pinpoint the exact source, then apply targeted repairs to stop leaks and prevent interior damage.

Missing Flashing

Flashing around chimneys, vents, and valleys is your roof's most vulnerable area. We install and replace flashing with precision, sealing every penetration point against wind-driven rain and snowmelt.

Granule Loss

Finding granules in your gutters? This signals accelerated shingle aging. We assess the extent of granule loss across your roof and replace shingles before UV damage weakens the underlying asphalt layer.
