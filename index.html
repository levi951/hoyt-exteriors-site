Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a roof replacement cost in Minnesota? +

A residential roof replacement in the Minneapolis-St. Paul metro typically ranges from $8,000 to $22,000 depending on roof size, pitch, material choice, and extent of decking damage. We provide free drone-inspected estimates with no obligation. Call (651) 212-4965 to schedule yours.

What areas do you serve in the Twin Cities? +

We serve Apple Valley, Burnsville, Eagan, Lakeville, Rosemount, Savage, Prior Lake, Farmington, Bloomington, Eden Prairie, Hastings, and surrounding South Metro communities. We also take select commercial and HOA projects across the greater Minneapolis-St. Paul metro.

Are you a GAF certified roofer? +

Yes — we're GAF Master Elite certified, which fewer than 1% of roofing contractors in the country achieve. This certification allows us to offer GAF's enhanced warranty coverage, including the System Plus and Golden Pledge warranties that provide superior protection for your investment.

Do you offer financing for roofing projects? +

Yes. We work with multiple financing partners to help homeowners and HOA communities manage large exterior projects. Ask about our current financing options when you call or request a quote — we'll match you with the best terms available for your project size.

How do drone inspections work and are they free? +

Our free drone inspections use high-resolution aerial photography and thermal imaging to document your entire roof and exterior from above. We identify damage, moisture intrusion, and deterioration that standard ground-level inspections miss. You receive a written report with photos — before any commitment to repair work.
