Siding Materials

The Right Siding for Every Home

We install a full range of siding materials — each selected for durability, beauty, and performance in Minnesota's demanding climate.

Vinyl Siding

The Way of the Future

Mastic, Vytec, and CertainTeed brands deliver low maintenance siding with excellent color retention. A wide range of profiles and colors make vinyl the most affordable option with outstanding ROI — perfect for homeowners who want lasting beauty without the upkeep.

Wood Siding

An Old Favorite

Nothing matches the natural beauty and timeless character of real wood. Cedar, pine, and redwood options provide excellent insulation properties and can be painted or stained any color — giving you complete creative control over your home's exterior.

Fiber Cement Siding

A Great Combination

The 2025 Remodeling Cost vs. Value Report shows fiber cement siding recoups approximately 88% of its cost at resale — one of the highest ROI exterior projects.

The best of both worlds — combining the durability of cement with the beauty of wood. Fire resistant, termite proof, and holds paint longer than natural wood. James Hardie and similar premium brands deliver lasting performance for Minnesota homes.

LP SmartSide

Always a Smart Choice

LP SmartSide carries a 5/50 year limited warranty — 5-year 100% labor and materials, 50-year prorated substrate coverage.

Louisiana Pacific's engineered wood siding is treated with SmartGuard technology to resist fungal decay, termites, and hail impact. Backed by a 5/50-year limited warranty, LP SmartSide combines the warmth of wood with the toughness your Minnesota home demands.

Steel Siding

Protection That Shines

The ultimate in durability and weather resistance. Steel siding won't crack, peel, or warp — and it's fire resistant too. Ideal for Minnesota's extreme weather, steel siding delivers decades of maintenance-free protection with a sleek, modern finish.
